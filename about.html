About Us

Our Story

Gym++ was founded in 2025 as a semester project by three Computer Science students. What started as a code project turned into a passion for creating a real community space. Located near HITEC University, we recognized the need for an affordable yet high-quality fitness center for students.


Our Mission

To provide a high-energy, inclusive environment where everyone—from beginners to pros—can exercise at an affordable price.

Our Vision

To become the leading fitness hub in Taxila, empowering the youth to prioritize health alongside their studies.
